About Me

MK Taylor Olsen

I'm a User Experience designer with a focus in big data and healthcare.

In my past life, I worked as an outdoor industry clothing designer and developer. It was during my time creating ski jackets and hiking pants, that I realized that placing trend and business desires over a user's need resulted in a poorly designed product. I found myself utilizing UX tools to understand our user's experiences. Through user interviews, competitive analysis and persona creation, we were able to develop a better product. I moved to the health care industry to continue solving problems through user-centric design and to make an impact. I typically work with projects from the initial user interviews all the way through front-end development (I love coding!)

When I'm not working, I can usually be found outdoors. Depending on the season, I'm either rock climbing, whitewater rafting, snowboarding or backpacking. Most likely with my husband, David.
